The Surface Is the Inside

The Bubble

Surface

A child stops everything for a soap bubble. There’s something revelatory about it — not just beautiful. As if it’s showing us something we already knew. It is.

Depth

The holographic principle — Bekenstein1, Hawking.2 All the information in a volume is encoded on its boundary. The interior is a projection of the surface. This isn’t metaphor. It’s physics. And it means interiority isn’t hidden behind the surface — it’s what the surface does. At every scale: cell membrane, skull, galactic boundary. Whitehead’s3 phrase fits exactly: the within of things is the boundary of things.
