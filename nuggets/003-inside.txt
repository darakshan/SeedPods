#depth
Whitehead's panpsychism is not the soft claim that maybe complex systems feel something. It is the stronger claim that experience is constitutive of what it means for any actual occasion to occur at all. If the process is real, the interiority is real — scaled and textured by the complexity of the entity, but not absent at any level. The question is not whether but what kind, and at which level of the nested hierarchy the relevant locus of experience resides.
