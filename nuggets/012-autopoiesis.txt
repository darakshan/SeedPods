#depth
Maturana and Varela's autopoiesis: a cell is not defined by the particular molecules it contains at any moment — those are constantly replaced. It is defined by the circular process through which it continuously produces the very components that sustain the process. The activity produces the boundary. The boundary sustains the activity. This is the Whiteheadian pattern achieving a new level of coherence — a society of occasions organized into a pattern stable enough to persist, flexible enough to respond, and sufficiently closed upon itself to have something like an interest in its own continuation.
