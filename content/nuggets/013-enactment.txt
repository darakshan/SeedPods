#brief
Evan Thompson's enactivism, building on Maturana and Varela: cognition is not the processing of representations of an external world inside a passive receiver. It is the enactment of a world — the organism and its environment co-arising through active engagement. A bacterium swimming toward a chemical gradient is not merely following a rule. It is enacting a world in which that gradient is meaningful. This is prehension at the scale of the organism — the Whiteheadian pattern in biological language.

#provenance
Thompson, E. Mind in Life (2007). Maturana, H. and Varela, F. The Tree of Knowledge (1987).
