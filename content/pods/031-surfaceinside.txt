#surface

#depth
The holographic principle — Bekenstein@ref(bekenstein, "Bekenstein (1972) — information content proportional to surface area, not volume."), Hawking.@ref(hawking, "Hawking, Stephen — black hole thermodynamics, boundary entropy.") All the information in a volume is encoded on its boundary. The interior is a projection of the surface. This isn't metaphor. It's physics. And it means interiority isn't hidden behind the surface — it's what the surface does. At every scale: cell membrane, skull, galactic boundary.
