# SeedPods
## Small ideas that change how you see. Start anywhere!

SeedPods is a collection of ideas that many of us will find surprising. This collection is alive, every changing, incomplete and growing.

Each seedpod is elaborated in several ways - a simple surface of the idea, a deep dive into it, references to works that go even further, a dramatic exposition, images (or for now, ideas for images).

The seedpods are organized into seven color-coded categories below. Click on any category to see its seedpods. You can find an overview of them all here: @link(0).
