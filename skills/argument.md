# How to compose an argument

The `@argument` subtitle captures the article's arc — not its topic but its reasoning movement.

## Rules

1. **One sentence** — not a tagline, not a paragraph. A somewhat long sentence is fine, but not artificially long.
2. **Captures the arc** — what the article *does*, not what it's *about*.
3. **Dense enough to stand alone** — a reader seeing only the argument should understand the article's essential claim.
4. **Inline markdown** — emphasis, bold, code, links all work.

## Word count

30–150 words. Defined in `config/settings.json`.

## Example (from pod 001)

> Every era has a framework so obvious it feels like seeing rather than thinking — caloric, the ether, the assumption that matter is dark inside — and the pattern by which such frameworks dissolve is specific, repeatable, and happening now.
