@section(depth)

Notice that @link(003) appears as a destination on three of the four trails. This is not an accident. The archive's central claim — that experience may be a fundamental feature of reality rather than a late biological anomaly — is where the epistemological, sensory, and cosmic investigations all converge. The AI trail arrives at the same territory from the other direction: not "does experience go all the way down?" but "does it extend to what we've built?"

The trails are not exhaustive. Many pods sit between the listed stops or off to the side. The @link(000) offers the explorer's approach. These trails offer the pilgrim's.
