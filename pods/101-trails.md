Every idea pod in the archive appears on at least one trail. @link(003) remains the gravitational center — it appears as a destination on the Epistemology, Sensation, and Cosmic trails, marking where the archive's investigations converge on the claim that experience may be fundamental rather than late. The five new trails — Frameworks, Perception, Boundary, Process, and Mandelbrot — cover the remaining pods, tracing how ideas spread and dissolve, how senses build experience from vibration, where inside meets outside, how complexity builds itself, and what one rule iterated actually produces. The @link(000) offers the explorer's approach. These trails offer the pilgrim's.
